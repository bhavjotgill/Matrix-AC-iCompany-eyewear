About

Company

Matrix Electronic India Pvt Ltd.

In 1998, Matrix Electronic Corporation opened its first interaction office in New Delhi. Since then, we are working to create a difference through our products, technology and of course services.

Welcome to Matrix Electronic India, your number one source for all electronics items especially Air Conditioners. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of AC, with a focus on mainly three characteristics, ie. being dependability, good customer service and lastly uniqueness.

Founded in 1990 by Bhavjot Gill, Matrix Hut(our first store) has come a long way from its beginnings in New Delhi. When Mr. Gill first started out, his passion for making a different and easy evironment providing the best equipment for his customers which drove him to do intense research, follow his passion, even quitting his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over India, and are thrilled to be a part of the upcoming new projects which give rise in the Electronic industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
    Bhavjot Singh Gill [CEO, Founder.]

Matrix Electronic India has grown to become a company offering a wide range of innovative and high-quality products for the Indian market. This includes products and solutions for Air Conditioners, Factory Automation and Industrial Systems, Semiconductor & Devices, Transportation Systems and Visual & Imaging.

The company was incorporated in the year 2010. Since then, we are consistently working to create a difference through our products
